MEDEVAC Dispatch Simulation

This NetLogo simulation examining different MEDEVAC dispatch policies was developed for the Army Medical Department Basic Officer Leadership Course (AMEDD BOLC). This is my second custom-made NetLogo simulation. NetLogo boils down to two concepts - turtles and patches. In this case, the turtles (objects) took the form of MEDEVACs casualties and medical treatment facilities (MTFs), and patches (locations) took the form of four provinces in Southern Afghanistan. Attached is a powerpoint detailing the input parameters, metrics, and different dispatch policies.
