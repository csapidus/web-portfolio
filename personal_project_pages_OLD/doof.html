The Doofinator Magic-Eye and Nixie Radio

It may sound silly, but this project was a childhood tribute to one of my favorite fictional inventors - Dr. Doofenshmirtz from Phineas and Ferb. In each episode Doofenshmirtz would develop a new maniacal invention, always knighted with the suffix "inator". On start-up and shut-down, this radio plays the Doofenshmirtz evil jingle (a running gag from the show). There are three switches - one for power, one for FM radio, and one for a playlist of songs sung by Doofenshmirtz (and friends). This was my first time messing with magic-eye tube, and a 6E5 was included with audio modulation. Fine-grid IN-8 nixie tubes light up the channel numbers for the FM radio. This project was a great way to get acquainted with different vacuum tubes, arduino shields, and general audio work.
