Classy Paduak IN-8 Nixie Clock

This classic nixie clock was a gift for my older brother Mohammad (Mo) Al-Husseini's birthday. The present also doubles as a thank you for his teaching me C++ over the course of a half-year. The combination of red wood, brass top, and black honeycomb mesh makes for a beautiful timepiece. The honeycomb mesh was actually taken from a replacement car grill purchased online, cut and twisted to shape. The brass top was cut to specification using a water-jet at the Georgia Tech Invention Studio.
