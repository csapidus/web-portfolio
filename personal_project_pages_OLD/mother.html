Folding Paduak Timepiece

This beautiful folding paduak timepiece was comissioned by an unnamed Georgia Tech student in Spring 2017. The three folding sections of the timepiece were all cut from the same piece of paduak. Indentations were made for both inserts and photographs. Rounded plexiglass covers the photographs. The paduak pieces were lacquered, and then hinged together for the final product.

Estimated Building Cost: $90.00
