Stained Dremel Carvings

This personal project was an experiment in basswood and the dremel tool. The cut basswood cost less than $5.00 and was purchased from Home Depot. I attempted an astronaut on the moon, taken partly from an online image. Once carved, the piece was stained and allowed to dry.

Estimated Building Cost Per: $10.00
