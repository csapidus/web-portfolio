6ATCH Nixie Graduation Cap

LEDs get boring, fast. What better way to showcase my time as an engineer than with a nixie graduation cap! This project has been in the works for a little over a year. This may be the world's first 200V+ powered mortarboard. Don't worry - the current is low! Safety is paramount. This project has gone through an incredible number of iterations, but ultimately, I decided to keep it fairly simple - a 6"x6" green PCB with a triple A battery pack, HVPS, and switch. As shown in some of the photos below, I experimented with cathode tubes in a 3D printed enclosure as well. The cathode tubes worked as expected, but the nixies were just so much cooler. I was forced to reach back to my electronics course notes to optimize how much juice was needed to ensure the cap can run through commencement.

Estimated Building Cost: $80.00
