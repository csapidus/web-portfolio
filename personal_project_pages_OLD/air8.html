Air8 Smart Cuff with Assembly

Fitting for a capstone, this project led to a number of firsts - first printed circuit board (PCB), first provisional patent, and first time driving motors. Team also included Aditya Muralidhar, Maher Saadeh, and Ayende Ibere. Client was Dr. Shivan Amin, an ENT from Dekalb Medical. Provisional patent filed, and a utility patent is likely.

Estimated Building Cost: $1075.00
