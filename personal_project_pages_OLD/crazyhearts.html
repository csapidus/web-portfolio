Crazy Hearts Cutting Board

This paduak, purpleheart, and maple cutting-board was a gift for Scott and Mona McDuff, who have been incredible pillars of support for my projects and I. I knighted this board Crazy Hearts, in part due to the wild arrangement of chckered colors, and in part due to the name of the primary wood utilized- purpleheart. To personalize the gift, "McDuffs" is inscribed in English in the top-right, and in Arabic in the bottom-left; this was done using Georgia Tech's Invention Studio laser cutter.
