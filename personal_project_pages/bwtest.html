Basswood Army Branch Insignia

These carved basswood pieces were developed as gifts for comissioning AROTC cadets Michael Khan (Signal), Joshua Miller (Aviation), Emily Solon (Medical), and Robby Jackson (Engineer). Branch insignias were placed on tracing paper, and then dotted with a dremel. A carving tool and dremel with assorted bits were then used to excavate the areas surronding the insignia. Each insignia was then stained, and the entirety of the piece sanded.
