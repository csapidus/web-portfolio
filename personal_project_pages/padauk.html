Padauk and Maple Cutting Board

This padauk and maple brick-style, laser-etched cutting board was made as a shared gift for my parents (mother's and father's day). This is was my first experience making a cutting-board. The most severe trap I fell to was failing to apply enough wood-glue, which was caught early enough to not be a problem. After cutting, gluing, planining, recutting, regluing, and sanding, the cutting board was laser etched with a simple message, and then coated with mineral oil. + Cat Tax
