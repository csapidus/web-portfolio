Acrylic Breakdancing Contraption

The BMED2310 final project required students to develop a contraption with at least three connected moving parts. I elected to model a breakdancer's legs as he prepares for a headspin. Unfortunately, the final product came out looking like more of an upside down crab. The legs are made of thin acrylic, the wood for the base is walnut, and the wood for the body is paduak.

Estimated Building Cost: $35.00
