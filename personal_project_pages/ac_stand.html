Acrylic On Padauk/Maple Stand

This project was a retirement gift for the loud, stubborn, and incredibly kind-hearted SFC Benslay. SFC Benslay was a deeply appreciated Georgia Tech cadre member. This was my first major foray into laser-etched acrylic. The stand was built with maple layered with paduak. The acrylic (3/8") was thicker than expected, and the hole in the stand had to be deepened a few times for best fit.

Estimated Building Cost: $75.00
