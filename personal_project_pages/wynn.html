Alphanumeric Nixie Nameplate

This walnut encased nixie "nameplate" was developed as a gift for CPT Wynn (soon to be MAJ Wynn), one of the Georgia Tech AROTC cadre. As of May 2016, CPT Wynn is preparing to depart for his next duty station, and this gift is a means of memorializing his efforts. A nixie tube is a cold cathode display containing a wire mesh anode. These nixie tubes were purchased from Ukraine, and were manufactured in the former Soviet Union. Eight IN-19 Nixie Tubes were utilized: °C, P, T, V,V, μ, n, n. Specifically, five IN-19As and three IN-19Bs were utilized in conjunction with a DIY high voltage power supply, 12V battery pack, and push-button switch.
