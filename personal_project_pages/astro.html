Astronomy Wall-Clock

This astronomy-based IN-18 nixie wall-clock with a walnut exterior was an incredibly fun to make personal project. A variety of skills were used, including the laser-cutting, hand engraving, and wood working. The IN-18 tubes are the largest of the traditional nixie tubes; as of winter 2016, each IN-18 runs for around 60.00 USD. A kit was utilized to ensure time-keeping capabilities. Behind the IN-18 tubes is a print of a stary night, taken from an old academic textbook. A NASA Space Shuttle blueprint is framed in the lower half of the clock using a thin piece of plexiglass secured with four gold nails. A rectangular piece of blue aluminum sits above the protected blueprint; on it is engraved a quote from the Quran on the humility of man. A rectangular piece of gold brass is secured to the very top of the wall-clock; on it is a message of thanks to the folks who assisted me in developing this clock.

Estimated Building Cost: $500.00
